Remember the used gloves from Thailand? It's gotten worse.

For those that need a refresher regarding the story, click here. Investigators have found more used medical items being sold to desperate US Hospitals. The items range from medication cups, IV tubing, urinals, and syringes. A CEO of a local hospital that wished to be anonymous, spoke out on the matter:

    "I regret to inform you that this story has affected our community. We are working with CDC officials to make sure those who have come into contact with these contaiminated items were not harmed during this time."

Much like the way SKYMED company dyed and washed the gloves, manufacturers bleached many of the items, leaving them brittle and breaking easily. In fact, this is what prompted investigators to search where these hospital items were shipped from.

US officials are working closely with Thailand authorities to conduct a criminal investigation on the matter.
